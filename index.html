Work Completed

Click to expand details
Assignment 1 — Quantum Gates

Studied matrix representations of gates and verified key identities (Pauli relationships, tensor products), reinforcing multi-qubit operations.

Assignment 2 — Simple Quantum Circuit Execution

Built and executed basic circuits using IBM’s platform; practiced gate placement, measurement, and interpreting results from real deployments.

Assignment 3 — QAOA Gate Construction

Analyzed QAOA cost and mixer operators and computed gates for small systems, building theoretical understanding of variational algorithms.

Current Work — Assignment 4 (QAOA for Max-Cut)

Translating QAOA operators into executable circuits, running simulations, and analyzing correctness and optimization strategies. This is our key transition from theory to practical algorithm development.
